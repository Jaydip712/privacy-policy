3. Internet Usage & External Links

Internet Permission

Bhakti Creation requires the android.permission.INTERNET permission. This permission allows the application to open external web pages using your device's default browser when you choose to view links such as the Privacy Policy or official website.

The app does not collect, store, or transmit personal user data through this permission. It is used only to allow access to publicly available web pages.

The App may require internet access for limited purposes:

  • When you open the Privacy Policy inside the App (which opens in your device browser)

These actions redirect you to external websites using your device’s default browser.

We:

Please note that external websites operate under their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.
